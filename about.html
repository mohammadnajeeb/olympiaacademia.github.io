About Us

The Olympia Academia is the Aligarh Muslim University Science Society, founded in 2019. Our purpose is to promote enjoyment and understanding of mathematics, physics, statistics and computer science for all students, and provide opportunities for cooperation and social activities./p>

Frequently, we organize talks here in the AMU, where students from all faculties come to talk for us on new or interesting topics in their area of expertise. These are a great way to be entertained or inspired. The talks are aimed at a general audience, so they are suitable for all. If you're curious, you can look at our upcoming and past events.

A major part of our society is to provide social events and gatherings for science students. We run plenty of events for our members to get together and mingle; like the Fresher's Squash right at the start of October. Further in, we have our Problems Drive.
